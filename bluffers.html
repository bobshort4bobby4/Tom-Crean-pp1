Ten Things to Know.

  • Born 20th July 1877 at Annascaul Co. Kerry
  • Joined Royal Navy 1893, 10 days short of his 16th birthday.
  • Shares his birthday with Sir Edmund Hillary, another great explorer.
  • Mount Crean in Antarctia is named after him.
  • The Crean glacier on South Georgia is named in his honour.
  • Was awarded the polar medal three times and the Albert medal.
  • Invalided from the Navy in March 1920, after nearly 27 years service.
  • Had three daughters Mary, Kate and Eileen.
  • Died from a perforated appendix in Cork 27 July 1938, aged 61.
  • Buried at Ballynacourty, in a tomb he built himself.
